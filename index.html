How to interpret results

Max Lyapunov λ — positive = chaos (exponential divergence). Estimated via Benettin renormalization: perturbation is periodically rescaled to stay small, accumulating the local stretching rate along the attractor.

Correlation dimension — non-integer = fractal. Integer (1 = cycle, 2 = torus) = regular. Grassberger-Procaccia algorithm on the plotted 3D trajectory.

Energy drift — for γ=0, H should be conserved. Large drift = numerical error.

Verdict — λ>0.1 AND non-integer dim = Strange attractor. λ>0.1 only = Chaotic. Non-integer only = Fractal. Neither = Not chaotic.
